Welcome to Samos Academy

My name is Santiago Giner, and I am a first-year student at Harvard College studying physics.

This is my tutoring website, where you can view the subjects I can teach and also schedule classes with me. I tutor in physics, math, computer science, and programming. I also specialize in standardized testing for college applications (in the US and abroad). I scored a 1520 on the SAT, 800 in the SAT Math Level 2, and 780 in the Physics SAT Subject Test. I am now in my second semester at Harvard, where I have taken classes in physics, math, computer science, and astrophysics. Do feel free to contact me at santiagoginer@college.harvard.edu with any and all questions regarding this website, my tutoring, or anything else you might have questions about. My goal is to teach as many people as I can, helping you achieve your academic goals, and offering advice on college in general.

About Me

Physics

You might not realize it, but physics and its achievements are all around you. For me, physics is one of the greatest achievements of humanity, which allows us to explain everything we see around us, from the behavior of subnuclear particles to the large-scale structure of stars and galaxy superclusters. Not only that, physics is behind all the major technological advancements of our era, including computers, wireless communication, batteries, transportation, and many others. I wish not only to teach you how to solve problems in physics and think analytically and critically, but I also want to teach you about the importance of physics, how it applies to our everyday life, and all the beauty it represents.
