Physics

You might not realize it, but physics and its achievements are all around you. For me, physics is one of the greatest achievements of humanity, which allows us to explain everything we see around us, from the behavior of subnuclear particles to the large-scale structure of stars and galaxy superclusters. Not only that, physics is behind all the major technological advancements of our era, including computers, wireless communication, batteries, transportation, and many others. I wish not only to teach you how to solve problems in physics and think analytically and critically, but I also want to teach you about the importance of physics, how it applies to our everyday life, and all the beauty it represents.

Mathematics

Mathematics is the basis of most everything that is done in modern science. It is the backbone of our understanding of the universe and it offers us a beautifully elegant framework upon which we can model real-world phenomena and make accurate predictions about them. Math is a language in it of itself, and speaking that language is one of the most useful skills one can learn, for it literally opens up a world of opportunites in which to work on and areas to explore. I hope to teach you not only mathematical concepts and abilities but also their real-world applications and importance.
